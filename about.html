I am a software engineer with a passion for big data.

This blog was started in October 2016 as part of my data science learning journey at General Assembly London.

I have explained in one of my first posts how much I admire Andrew Ng's teaching style and in particular his habit to always describe what he calls the intuition before going further into details. By intuition he means the core idea, the extreme simplification of a concept of phenomena that once undestood makes the rest of the learning process much simpler.

My main motivation for this blog is to help anyone interested in data science grasp some of the core concepts by explaining the intuition behind these concepts. I will focus on topics that I found challenging to understand during my own learning journey.
